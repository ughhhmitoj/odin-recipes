Chilli Paneer

Description

Chilli Paneer is a well-known Indo-Chinese dish. Even for beginners, the recipe for chilli paneer is quite simple and easy. Paneer is first coated in a cornstarch batter and fried until crisp. Then, ingredients such as garlic, capsicum, and spring onions are sautéed to make a fresh chilli sauce. The sauce is then made by combining red chilli sauce, soy sauce, and vinegar. Serve with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 300 gms Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • 2 tblsp finely chopped Ginger
  • 2 tblsp finely chopped Garlic
  • 3 – 4 Green Chillies Slit Lengthwise
  • 1 Green Capsicum Diced
  • 1 Onion Diced
  • 1 – 2 finely chopped Spring Onions
  • 2 tsp Red Chilly Sauce
  • Ajinomoto (Monosodium Glutamate)(Optional)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tblsp Vinegar
  • 1 tblsp Cornflour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 tblsp finely chopped Green coriander

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes.
  2. Take all the ingredients under the marinade section. Mix well. Now with the help of cold water make thick batter. The batter should not be drippy.
  3. Now add the paneer cubes. When you hold the marinated paneer the batter should not drip. If it does than add some extra maida to make it thick. Mix well and keep it in the fridge for half an hour
  4. After take out the marinated paneer and fry them golden brown and keep aside.
  5. Take the cornflour under the main heading and make slurry of it by adding 1 – 2 tblsp of water. Make sure there are no lumps.
  6. In a wok, heat the oil , on high flame add the ginger garlic chopped. Stir fry for 1 min or till they get slightly brown.
  7. Now add the diced onions, sliced green chillies and green capsicum.
  8. Stir fry for a min and then add red chilly sauce, ajinomoto, salt, pepper, soya sauce.
  9. Mix well and add 2 – 3 tblsp of hot water.
  10. Add the prepared cornflour slurry.
  11. Let the sauce thicken.
  12. Add the fried paneer cubes and add vinegar.
  13. Once the paneer begins to thicken switch off the flame and garnish with spring onions and finely chopped green coriander.
